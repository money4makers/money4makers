1. What affiliate marketing means

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based arrangement where we earn a small commission when you click a link on our site and complete a purchase or sign-up on a third-party platform. The commission comes from the merchant — not from you. You pay the same price whether or not you use our link.

This is one of the primary ways Money4Makers.com generates revenue, which allows us to keep all of our guides, strategies, and newsletter content free for everyone.

2. Which links may be affiliate links

Any link on Money4Makers.com that points to a third-party product, tool, platform, or service may be an affiliate link. This includes links in:

  • Articles, guides, and how-to content.
  • Tool reviews and comparisons.
  • Our weekly newsletter.
  • The tier breakdown and tool recommendations on our homepage.

We do not mark every individual affiliate link with a separate disclosure label, but this page serves as our site-wide disclosure in compliance with FTC guidelines.

4. Our editorial standards
