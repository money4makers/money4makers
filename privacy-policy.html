Effective date: April 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 4, 2026

Plain English summary: Money4Makers.com collects your email address when you sign up for our newsletter or free guide. We use it only to send you content you asked for. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who we are

Money4Makers.com ("we," "us," or "our") is a website that publishes practical guides, strategies, and resources to help everyday people generate income online. This Privacy Policy applies solely to the website located at money4makers.com and governs the collection, use, and protection of your personal data on that website only.

For all privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at: privacy@money4makers.com

2. What data we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us. Currently, this is limited to:

  • Your email address, when you sign up for our newsletter or request our free guide.

We do not collect your name, phone number, payment information, or any other personal data unless you explicitly provide it to us through direct contact.

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to your personal identity.

3. How we use your data

We use your email address solely to:

  • Send you the free guide or resource you requested.
  • Deliver our weekly newsletter, which contains practical money-making strategies and tools.
  • Occasionally notify you of new guides, products, or resources we believe are relevant to you.

We will never use your email address for any purpose beyond what you signed up for without your explicit consent.

4. How we store your data

Your email address is stored securely through Beehiiv, our newsletter platform. Beehiiv maintains industry-standard security practices and is compliant with applicable data protection regulations. You can review Beehiiv's privacy practices at beehiiv.com/privacy.

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. If you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list within 30 days.

5. Cookies and tracking

Money4Makers.com uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. We present a cookie consent notice when you first visit the site, and you may manage your preferences at any time. These technologies include:

  • Essential cookies required for the site to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use the site (aggregated and anonymized).

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that some site features may not function properly if essential cookies are disabled.

Money4Makers.com contains affiliate links to third-party products and services. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review before providing any personal information.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any third-party websites linked from money4makers.com.

7. We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. Your data is used exclusively to provide you with the content and services you requested from Money4Makers.com.

8. Your rights — United States (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@money4makers.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

9. Your rights — European Union (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • The right to request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your personal data.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

Our legal basis for processing your email address is your explicit consent, given when you submitted your email address through our signup form. You may withdraw this consent at any time by unsubscribing from our newsletter or contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@money4makers.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

10. Children's privacy

Money4Makers.com is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. This age threshold satisfies both the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the European Union's GDPR, which sets the digital consent age at 16 in most member states.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. By submitting your email address through our signup form, you affirm that you are 16 years of age or older. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a person under 16, we will delete it promptly. To report such a concern, contact us at privacy@money4makers.com.

11. Accessibility

Money4Makers.com is committed to digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA standard as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). If you experience any accessibility barriers on our website, please contact us at privacy@money4makers.com and we will make every reasonable effort to resolve the issue promptly.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of money4makers.com after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
